Rules of the OI Diphthong Game

  • Objective of the Game: Correctly complete the words that contain the diphthong "OI" to earn points.
  • Saying the Word Correctly: After guessing the word correctly, the team must say the word; otherwise, they will not receive the point they earned.
  • Use of Hints:
    • The player can request a hint that consists of the pronunciation of the complete word. Only one hint is allowed per word.
  • Point System:
    • Each time the player correctly completes a word, they receive 1 point.
    • If the player completes the word incorrectly, they do not receive points and lose 1 life.
  • Lives:
    • Each team starts with a total of 3 lives.
    • If the team loses all their lives, the game ends.
  • Time Limit:
    • The bomb can explode at any moment, so think fast!
